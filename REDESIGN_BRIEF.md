# 🎨 PrimeBets Redesign 2025 - Master Brief

## 1. Vision & Core Identity

**Positioning:** Premium horse racing intelligence. NOT a betting site.
**Vibe:** Bloomberg × Rolex × modern AI SaaS.
**Feel:** Analytical, Calm, Premium, Trustworthy, Confident.
**User Sentiment:** "These are experts. This is serious. I can trust this."

## 2. Visual DNA & Color System (LOCKED)

**Colors:**
- Primary Layout Background: `#0F1720`
- Card Surface: `#162230` (Opacity 85–92%)
- Overlay/Dim: `#0C131C` (Opacity 55–65%)
- Primary Text: `#E6ECF2`
- Secondary Text: `#9AA7B5`
- Muted Metadata: `#7C8A98`
- Primary Accent: `#2FAE8F` (Forest green, no neon)
- Accent Dark: `#248E75`
- Accent Glow: `#43C4A2` (Max 25% opacity, very subtle)
- Gold Accent: `#C9A86A` (For stakes/units only)

**Typography:**
- Modern, clean, technical sans-serif.
- Strong hierarchy via weight.
- Tone: Analyst briefing, not sales pitch. No emojis.

**Global Principles:**
- No pure black/white.
- Glassmorphism (semi-transparent cards).
- Subtle backgrounds (stadium lights, bokeh, depth gradients).

## 3. Card Archetypes

### 1. LÖRDAGENS DRAG (Saturday Pick)
- **Role:** The Star. Emotion + Spotlight.
- **Visuals:** Premium horse image (Large, cinematic, cropped). Dark overlay.
- **Vibe:** Powerful, controlled premium.

### 2. DAGENS PRIMEPICK
- **Role:** Decision Clarity.
- **Visuals:** NO horse image. Clean, data-first.
- **Content:** Horse name, location, method, distance, track, bet type, stake (units), odds, value %, CTA.

### 3. VECKANS SPANING
- **Role:** Insight & Observation.
- **Visuals:** NO full horse. Horse legs ONLY (running in dirt). Abstract/motion.
- **Vibe:** "Analytical observation".

### 4. SENASTE HETA
- **Role:** Proof of Performance.
- **Visuals:** NO images. Text/Data only.
- **Content:** ROI, # bets, avg odds, lists. "Bloomberg snapshot".

### 5. INTRESSANTA SPEL
- **Role:** Overview.
- **Visuals:** Minimal. List-based.

## 4. Implementation Strategy
- **Phase 1:** Build "Fake" Prototype at `/v2-preview`.
- **Phase 2:** Iterate design until perfect.
- **Phase 3:** Connect to real Supabase data.
- **Phase 4:** Launch.
